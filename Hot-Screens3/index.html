two screens twenty-four hours

Our Laptops will take screenshots of whatever we are doing every 10 minutes for 2 Days. For this we have installed Tools to automate the process of taking the screenshots. The screenshot will be squeezed so it’s only a line of information. These distorted narrow screenshots build up a timeline. The pictures can be investigated further by hovering over them. The html site will have indicators so the visitors know what they can do. The Intent of this project is to show what we are doing on our laptops, and give a glimpse of our daily screen time of working days. It can be seen as a comparison between us.
